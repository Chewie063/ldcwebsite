About Us

Welcome to Legacy Dance Center

At Legacy Dance Center we strive to shape and develop creative, confident dancers. Dancers are taught technique, teamwork and the art of dance in a structured and positive environment. We strive to bring out the very best in each dancer and encourage their unique individual personalities. All our students develop as individuals while we remain a "dance family."

Our goal is for our littlest dancers LOVE the art of dance and have fun and our dancers to grow and use the lessons they learn from dance class to become well-rounded GOOD humans and dancers.

This is Legacy's 10th year and over 25 years of experience in the dance industry. We promise to always give your child the best experience in each dance class so they leave with positive energy and a confidence boost! Welcome to our dance family!

Class Descriptions

Preschool Combo
(3&4 year olds)

An introductory program designed to enhance rhythm skills, coordination, musicality, and a genuine love for the art of dance. Basic ballet and tap instruction is taught in this one hour class.

Hip Hop

The millennium version of "street dance" performed to today's most popular music. Learning body isolations, tricks, and sharper movements to move with a large group to look like one.

Jazz

Jazz is a dance form that appeals to everyone because of its energy, variety, and vitality. Classes explore body isolations, technique in turns and jumps, and encourage individual expression and the development of personal style. Jazz can be powerful and percussive or expressive and slow moving.
